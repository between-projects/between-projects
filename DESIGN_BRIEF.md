# Design Brief

## Overview

**between-projects** is a studio dedicated to connection and growth. We create digital experiences that bridge ideas with execution, focusing on meaningful design that serves both aesthetics and purpose.

---

## Mission

To foster connection and growth through thoughtful design, strategic thinking, and purposeful technology.

## Vision

A digital landscape where design serves as a bridge—connecting people to ideas, businesses to audiences, and creativity to impact.

---

## Core Values

### Connection
Design should create pathways between people and ideas. Every interface is an opportunity to build understanding and engagement.

### Growth
We believe in continuous learning and evolution. Our work reflects an ongoing commitment to improvement and adaptation.

### Intentionality
Every design decision serves a purpose. We avoid decoration for its own sake in favor of meaningful, functional elements.

### Clarity
Communication should be clear and accessible. We strip away complexity to reveal essential meaning.

---

## Design Philosophy

### Human-Centered
We design for people first. Technology and aesthetics serve the user experience, not the other way around.

### Systems Thinking
Individual components exist within larger systems. We consider context, relationships, and scalability in every decision.

### Progressive Enhancement
Start with solid foundations and layer complexity thoughtfully. Core experiences should work everywhere, enhanced experiences reward capable environments.

### Sustainable Design
We create solutions meant to last. This means accessible code, maintainable systems, and timeless aesthetics over trends.

---

## Target Audience

### Primary
- Small to medium businesses seeking digital presence
- Creative professionals needing portfolio and identity systems
- Organizations requiring communications strategy

### Secondary
- Developers seeking collaboration on design-forward projects
- Designers interested in design systems and web patterns
